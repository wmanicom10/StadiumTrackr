General

What is StadiumTrackr?

StadiumTrackr is a platform for keeping track of the stadiums you've visited and discovering new ones to explore. Log your visits with notes and dates, add stadiums you want to visit to your wishlist, earn achievements as you reach milestones, and see upcoming events at each stadium.

Is it free?

Yes, StadiumTrackr is free to use, but some features are only available to Pro members. See the Pro page for more details on upgrading.

Do I need an account to use the site?

No, you can browse stadiums without an account, but you'll need an account to track your visits.

How do I use StadiumTrackr?

For a more comprehensive guide on how to use StadiumTrackr, visit our About page. This covers the most common ways to navigate the site and get the most out of your experience.

Do you have a mobile app?

Our mobile app is currently in development, stay tuned for updates.

Account

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, visit the Settings page, navigate to the Delete Account tab, and click the Delete Account Button. Once you do this, you'll be asked to enter your password to confirm. After this is done, your account and all of your data will be permanently deleted.

Can I change my username/password/email/profile picture?

Yes, you can change your username, password, email, and profile picture, all on the Settings page.

What happens if I forgot my password?

No worries, just visit our Forgot Password page where you'll enter your email to get a link to reset your password.

Why should I upgrade my account?

StadiumTrackr relies on membership fees as our main source of income, so if you like StadiumTrackr and want to help us out, feel free to support us by upgrading. For a full list of benefits to upgrading your account, visit the Pro page.

Using StadiumTrackr

What's the difference between marking a stadium as visited and logging a stadium?

Marking a stadium as visited just says that you've been to that stadium at some point, without specifying a date. Logging a stadium visit lets you specify the date you visited the stadium, and lets you add notes to remember your visit. All logged visits show up on your Visits page so you can see your visits over time.

How do I unmark a stadium as visited?

To unmark a stadium as visited, you can toggle the visit icon on a stadium card when hovering on it, or visit the stadium page and unmark it as visited. Before unmarking as visited however, you'll need to delete any visit logs for that stadium first.

Can I log a stadium visit and change the date and/or note later?

Yes, you can edit a stadium visit by clicking on the edit button on either the Visits or Activity Page.

How do I add a stadium to my wishlist?

To add a stadium to your wishlist, you can either hover on a stadium card and toggle the wishlist icon, or visit a stadium page and use the wishlist button.

What are favorite stadiums?

You can add up to 4 favorite stadiums, and they can be stadiums you visit the most, stadiums for your favorite teams, or anything you want. By setting a stadium as one of your favorites, it lets you see upcoming events for that stadium on your Events page.

How do I see other users' accounts?

Currently, there is no way to see other users' accounts, but we plan on adding this feature in the future.

Events

How do I see upcoming events at the stadiums I want?

By setting stadiums as one of your favorites, or adding stadiums to your wishlist, you can see the next 3 events at those stadiums on your Events page.

Where do you get event data for each stadium?

All of the event data on StadiumTrackr comes from Ticketmaster, so it is always up-to-date.

Why should I buy tickets through StadiumTrackr?

When you purchase tickets through our Ticketmaster link, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. It's a simple way to support us while buying tickets you were already going to buy.

Data

Where does StadiumTrackr get its data?

All stadium data on StadiumTrackr is manually collected, and we are always working to add more stadiums. If you notice any issues with stadium information or would like to request a stadium to be added, please contact data@stadiumtrackr.com.

What leagues does StadiumTrackr cover?

Right now, StadiumTrackr has every stadium from the following leagues:

  • NFL
  • NBA
  • MLB
  • NHL
  • MLS
  • WNBA
  • NCAAF

We are always adding more stadiums and leagues, so stay tuned if you don't see the stadium you're looking for.

Achievements

How do I earn achievements?

Achievements are unlocked automatically as you reach different milestones. Both marking a stadium as visited and logging a stadium visit contributes to achievement progress, but some achievements rely on date-specific logs.
