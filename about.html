About

Track every stadium you've visited.

With StadiumTrackr, you can mark every stadium you've been to as visited, log when you went, add stadiums to your wishlist, and see upcoming events at stadiums around the world.

Tell us where you've been

Visit the stadiums page and mark stadiums you've been to as visited. Every stadium you've been to shows up on your My Stadiums page.

Add stadiums to your wishlist

Adding stadiums to your wishlist lets you keep track of stadiums you want to visit in the future, and lets you see upcoming events at those stadiums on the Events page.

Log your visits

Log the date you visited a stadium and add notes to remember the experience. Each visit counts as an event and shows up in your stats and activity timeline.

Never miss a game

Follow upcoming games at your favorite stadiums and stadiums in your wishlist. All event data comes straight from Ticketmaster, and you can buy tickets for any event with one click.

Visualize your stadium journey

Every stadium you visit shows up on the map on your home page, so you can see where in the world you've been.

Earn achievements

Unlock achievements as you explore stadiums across different sports, leagues, states, and countries. Track your progress and see how close you are to your next achievement.
